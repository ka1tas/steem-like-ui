TF2 Glitches: Strange Uniques

A Strange Unique item is an item in TF2 that behaves like a Strange item, yet has yellow text like a unique item. The item shows up in your backpack with the standard strange counter icon in the upper right corner, and does indeed keep count. The majority of items that were produced from this glitch originated from crate unboxings, but the glitch affected all items that should have been Strange, including MvM rewards. A handful of Strange Unique Australiums and Botkillers were created as a result.
